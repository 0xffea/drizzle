Logical and Comparison Operators
================================

=========     ==========================
Operators     Description
=========     ==========================
AND (&&)       Logical AND
OR (||)        Logical OR
NOT (!)        Logical NOT
< 	       less than
> 	       greater than
<= 	       less than or equal to
>= 	       greater than or equal to
= 	       equal
<> or !=       not equal
=========     ==========================
